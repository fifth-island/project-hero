Key Research Findings: Synthesis

  • 1

    Solar Heating Dominance

    WBGT peaks consistently at 17:00, trailing solar noon by 5 hours, driven by the thermal mass of asphalt and masonry.

  • 2

    Scavenging Mechanisms

    The weekend PM2.5 elevation (+1 µg/m³) indicates that weekday traffic actually scavenges ozone and particulates via NO titration.

  • 3

    Wildfire Confounding

    Episodic events, such as the Wednesday wildfire spike, can skew seasonal averages by up to 15% if not temporally isolated.

  • 4

    Compound Exposure Risks

    The 3:00-6:00 PM window represents a 'perfect storm' where both thermal stress and particulate load are at concurrent local maxima.
