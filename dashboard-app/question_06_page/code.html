Environmental audit confirming the systemic nature of spatial disparities during hazardous climate events.

Trigger Analysis

The Aug 19 peak was driven by an external wildfire smoke plume. However, local wind stagnation (2.1 mph) acted as the primary spatial differentiator, trapping particulates in urban canyons.

Structural Equity
