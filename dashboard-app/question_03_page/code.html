Core Research Finding

The distribution analysis confirms a critical environmental injustice: nearly half (46.3%) of all PM2.5 readings in the Chinatown study area exceed the EPA’s annual NAAQS health threshold of 9.0 µg/m³. This represents a persistent exposure level that significantly outpaces regional Boston averages.
