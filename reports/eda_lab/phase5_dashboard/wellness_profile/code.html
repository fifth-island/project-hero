The 'Selection Effect' Paradox

While raw data suggests that STEM majors report higher stress levels, a deeper longitudinal analysis reveals the Selection Effect. Students with higher pre-existing grit often self-select into more rigorous programs. However, the data confirms that Study Time—not the discipline itself—is the primary driver of wellness degradation.
